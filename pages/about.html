About TUDEMM LLC

A digital-native firm built to help you compete.

We're a digital products creator and professional services firm in Worthington, Ohio — on a mission to help small and mid-sized organizations thrive in an increasingly digital marketplace.

Our story

Closing the digital readiness gap.

TUDEMM LLC is a digital products creator and professional services firm headquartered at 1121 Worthington Woods Blvd., Suite 1031, Worthington, Ohio 43085. We design and build professional websites and deliver search engine optimization services that help small and mid-sized organizations compete in an increasingly digital marketplace.

As a digital-native company operating in the Columbus metro corridor, we understand that many capable businesses are held back not by their talent or ambition, but by a gap in digital readiness. We exist to close that gap — pairing professional services with practical digital products so every organization we work with can show up, stand out, and grow online.

Our mission

Two commitments guide everything we do.

1

Helping entrepreneurs harness digital tools

2

For the small businesses we serve across the Columbus metro and beyond, we make modern digital practice accessible and achievable.
